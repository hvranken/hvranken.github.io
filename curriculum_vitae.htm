Curriculum vitae Harald Vranken


Education

  • 1981 - 1987: VWO (Gymnasium-B), Sgs. Jeanne d'Arc, Maastricht
  • 1987 - 1988: Electrical Engineering (propaedeutic), Eindhoven University of Technology
  • 1988 - 1992: Information Technology (ir.), Eindhoven University of Technology
  • 1992 - 1994: Information- and Communication Technology (PDEng), Eindhoven University of Technology
  • 1994 - 1998: Ph.D., Faculty of Electrical Engineering, Eindhoven University of Technology
  • 2005 - 2006: Science Education (MSc), Eindhoven University of Technology

Work

  • 1998 - 2005: Senior scientist, Philips Research, Eindhoven
  • 2005 - 2006: Mathematics teacher, Bisschoppelijk College Weert
  • 2006 - 2012: Assistant professor, School of Computer Science, Open Universiteit
  • 2012 - 2023: Associate professor, Open Universiteit (Faculty of Science)
  • 2023 - current: Full professor in cyber security, Open Universiteit (Faculty of Science)

  • Since 2014 partly seconded (0.15 fte) to Radboud University (Faculty of Science)
